Eve's Vision:

Eve’s balance is a one-stop shop for scheduling loc appointments and purchasing handmade holistic/spiritual creations meant to ground you and feel light, positive energy. The Logo is a black woman with succulents embedded in her hair, succulents symbolizes endurance, strength, beauty, love and resilience. These things are healthy in balance. “Eve’s Balance Slogan: Beauty Rooted in Care, Energy, and Style—Where Locs and Holistic Creations Unite to Embrace Your Essence.”
